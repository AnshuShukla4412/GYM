    WHAT'S SS PRO ?

    Inspired by the Big Apple, on the pulse of the Big Smoke, we redefine the modern-day workout with our signature SS PRO . We capture Agra’s infectious energy, tailored to Uttar Pradesh lifestyles.

    By coming to SS PRO , you do workouts which will keep your body in a proper shape and your body will remain fit. In SS PRO , you will get all the tools required for body workouts.

    You also get a trainer here and you can workout from Monday to Saturday and rest on Sunday because SS PRO is closed on Sunday. You will have to do different exercises daily during the week which will be trained by the trainers available here.

    Energized by Endorphins, Shaped by Innovation.

    Our endurance-focused SS PRO workout is designed to target slow-twitch muscle fibers, boosting both strength and metabolism.

    While intense, the workout is easy on the joints, incorporating a series of resistance-based, mindful movements that are intensified to deliver transformational results. We call it Transformer Pilates.

    At the heart of our philosophy is the belief that a strong mind-body connection is key to achieving lasting results and surpassing personal goals. Each session concludes with five minutes of endorphin-boosting mindfulness and breathwork, helping to center the mind and prepare our clients to navigate the demands of modern life.
